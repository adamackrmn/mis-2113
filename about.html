About

The course educates students on how information technology facilitates organizations to achieve its goals and solve problems.

Students will learn to use latest tools of information technology, conduct hands-on-exercises and solve problems.

Hence, students will become familiar with advanced use of spreadsheet and database software, networking technologies, web and application programming languages, and business analytics methods
